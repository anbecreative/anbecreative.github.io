Philips Accessory packaging

Art direction and packaging guidelines

Design and create packaging guideline system that unites all Philips accessory products under one look in retail.

As so many different packs are being created by many printers and suppliers it was getting difficult for product managers to keep a consistent look and feel.

Once these guidelines were available I briefed all design suppliers and ensured that all packs would be consistent when seen together. This was well received by the many product managers and reduced time required to bring new products to market.
